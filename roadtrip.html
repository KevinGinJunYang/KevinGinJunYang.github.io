Roadie

Fuel cost application for NZ roadtrips.

Dash your way through and survive! How long can you survive? Dash your way through the incoming enemies and claim your bragging rights as a slime! the longer you survive the greater the score.
- The longer you survive the harder it gets
- Push your reactions to the limit.

Blob Dash is a acade type game that has a simple concept around dodging the incoming enemies. It was made using the Unity engine and was a fantastic project to work and learn on.
