CarFix is changing the way people get WoFs / service their cars.  We do this by picking up and dropping off your car from a location of your choice and time, we then advocate on your behalf to give you a fair deal and make sure only the necessary repair work is done.  We send you regular updates, so you know exactly what is happening to your car.

At CarFix we ensure that there is no risk for you when you use our services. We only use MTA(Motor Trade Association) approved mechanics so that you know you're getting quality service on your vehicle and that they are legitimate mechanics that are professionally trained in this field of expertise.

We also run thorough background checks on our drivers including their criminal records  to make sure they are trustworthy and honest people so that you know your car is safe with us. We also have insurance to cover your car for any damage that may happen to your vehicle!

Carfix gives you constant real time updates on what's happening with your vehicle. This includes any extra repairs that it might need or  answer any questions you may have. Our customer support team is ready to answer your questions so that you know your're getting what you paid for. All details are explained before and after the job has been completed.
