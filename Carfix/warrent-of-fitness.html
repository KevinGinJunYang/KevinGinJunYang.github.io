What is a Warrant of Fitness?

A warrant of fitness (WoF) is a regular check to make sure that your vehicle meets road safety standards. It is required to have one to drive on the roads. It is your job to make sure that your car always has a valid Warrant of fitness or else you may be penalized to receiving a fine.

When do you need a Warrant?

Vehicles register time

  • New vehicles that's never been registered
  • Vehicles that where registered less than 2 years ago
  • More than 2 years ago but less than 3 years
  • On or after January 2000
  • Before January 2000

WoF issue time

  • Lasts 3 years before needing a new WoF
  • WoF is issued to vehicles third 'birthday' of when it was registered
  • WoF is issued for 12 months before needing new WoF
  • WoF is issued for 12 months before needing a new WoF
  • WoF is issued for 6 months before needing a new WoF

What does the Warrant of Fitness check cover?

  • Tyre condition (including tread depth)
  • brake operation
  • structural condition (rust not allowed in certain areas)
  • lights (must be funtional and working at correct brightness)
  • gazing (window screen safe)
  • windscreen washers and wipers
  • Doors(do they open and close properly and safely)
  • airbags(if fitted)
  • speedometer(must be working)
  • steering and suspension(must be safe and secure)
  • exhaust(must be no leaks and exhaust must not be smokey or loud)
  • fuel system(must be no leaks)
